Rules and Regulation

Players

A match is played by two teams, each consisting of not more than eleven players,one of whom is the goalkeeper

Rules of substitution

1.)The referee is informed before any proposed substitution is made.
2.)A substitute only enters the field of play after the player being replaced has left and after recieving a signal from the referee
3.)A subsstitute only enters the field of play at the halfway line and during a stoppage in a match

Penalty kicks

A penalty kick is awarded against a team which commits one of the six offences for which a direct free kick is awarded, inside its own penalty area and while the ball is in play.
